Section 1: Setting Up Your Etsy Shop

Etsy is perfect for beginners—free to start, instant access to millions of buyers, and simple to use. Follow these steps to launch your shop.

1

Go to Etsy Seller Portal

Visit etsy.com/sell and click "Get Started"

2

Create Your Account

Email: bampmediasa@gmail.com

Set a strong password and verify your email

3

Set Shop Preferences

  • Language: English
  • Country/Region: United States
  • Currency: USD ($)
4

Name Your Shop

Shop Name: BAMPPrints (no spaces)

This will appear in your Etsy URL: etsy.com/shop/BAMPPrints

5

Add Your First Listing

Use the Etsy_Product_Listings.xlsx file for all product information:

  • Photos: Take high-quality photos of your printed kits, or use placeholder product images for now
  • Title: Copy exactly from the spreadsheet
  • Description: Copy from the spreadsheet with formatting
  • Tags: Copy from spreadsheet (maximum 13 tags per listing)
  • Price: Use the pricing listed in the spreadsheet
  • Shipping: Set up USPS Priority Mail shipping profiles
6

Set Shop Policies

  • Returns: 30 days for defective items
  • Processing Time: 3-7 business days
  • Shipping Discount: Free shipping on orders over $150
7

Set Up Payment

Use Etsy Payments (their default payment processor). They handle everything—no separate setup needed. Payouts go directly to your bank account.

8

Add Shop Announcement

Announcement text: "Welcome to BAMP Prints! Use code FIRSTFLIGHT for 10% off!"

This appears at the top of your shop page to new visitors

9

Publish and Go Live!

Review everything and publish your shop. Your first listing will be visible to Etsy's millions of buyers immediately.

Etsy Setup Checklist

Etsy Fees to Remember:
• $0.20 per listing (one-time)
• 6.5% transaction fee (per sale)
• 3% + $0.20 payment processing fee (per sale)

Section 3: Recommended Multi-Channel Strategy

The best businesses don't rely on just one sales channel. Here's the recommended approach for BAMP Prints:

Launch Timeline

Week 1: Start with Etsy

Launch your Etsy shop first. It's free to list, you get instant access to millions of buyers, and the setup takes only 30 minutes. Start selling immediately.

Week 1-2: Build Shopify in Parallel

Use Shopify's free trial (and $1/month after) to build your branded store while selling on Etsy. Add the same products and optimize your store during the trial.

Week 3: Launch Shopify

Connect your domain and go live with your branded store. Start promoting it on social media and to existing customers. Both stores run simultaneously.

Month 2+: Add Instagram Shop

Once established, add an Instagram Shop to reach social media users. This ties into your Shopify inventory and reaches a different audience.

Why This Strategy Works

Etsy: Instant buyer access, discovery engine, lower barrier to entry

Shopify: Branded experience, higher margins (you own the customer), better for repeat buyers

Together: Maximize revenue from multiple sources while building your brand

Margin Comparison

Channel Fees Your Margin
Etsy 9.5%+ (transaction + processing) ~90%
Shopify 3.2% (payment only) ~96%
Pro Tip: Run both simultaneously. Etsy drives discovery and traffic. Shopify builds your brand and improves margins on repeat customers. The small Shopify investment ($1-39/month) quickly pays for itself with just 1-2 extra sales per month.
