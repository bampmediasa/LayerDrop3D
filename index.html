Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are the most common ones.

We accept STL, OBJ, and 3MF files. If you have a different format, reach out and we'll see if we can work with it. For RC airplane kits, you don't need to send any files — just tell us which design you want and we'll handle the rest.
We offer PLA, LW-PLA (lightweight foaming PLA — ideal for RC planes), PETG, and TPU. Our RC airplane kits are printed in LW-PLA by default for the best weight-to-strength ratio. For custom jobs we'll recommend the best material for your project.
Most jobs are printed and shipped within 3-7 business days depending on complexity. RC airplane kits may take 5-10 days as they have many parts. We'll give you an estimated timeline when you submit your order.
For our recommended designs from 3DAeroventures, you can download the free files for your personal use directly from their website. Then either send us the files to print, or simply tell us which design you want and we'll handle everything. The designs are free — you're paying for our printing and shipping service.
You'll need electronics to complete your RC airplane: a motor, ESC (electronic speed controller), servos, propeller, battery, and a radio transmitter/receiver. Each design page on 3DAeroventures includes recommended components. We can help point you in the right direction.
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal through our secure payment processor. You'll receive an invoice with a payment link after we confirm your order details and pricing.
