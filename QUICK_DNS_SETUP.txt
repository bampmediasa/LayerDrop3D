NAMECHEAP DNS SETUP FOR layerdrop3d.com
========================================

QUICK COPY-PASTE GUIDE (2 MINUTES):

1. Log into Namecheap: https://www.namecheap.com/
2. Click "Domain List" (left sidebar)
3. Click "Manage" next to layerdrop3d.com
4. Click "Advanced DNS" tab

5. DELETE any existing A records (click trash icon)

6. ADD THESE 4 A RECORDS (click "Add new record" each time):
   Type: A        Host: @    Value: 185.199.108.153
   Type: A        Host: @    Value: 185.199.109.153
   Type: A        Host: @    Value: 185.199.110.153
   Type: A        Host: @    Value: 185.199.111.153

7. ADD THIS CNAME RECORD:
   Type: CNAME    Host: www  Value: bampmediasa.github.io

8. Click "Save All Changes" (green button at top)

9. Wait 10-15 minutes for DNS to propagate

10. Test: Visit https://layerdrop3d.com (should show your website)
